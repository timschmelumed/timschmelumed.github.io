Categorical Perception

This experiment tests how you perceive speech sounds along two continua: /ba/→/da/ and /ra/→/la/. You will complete an identification task and an AXB discrimination task. Your results will be plotted at the end.

Identification: You hear a sound and decide which phoneme it is.

AXB Discrimination: You hear three sounds (A, X, B) and decide whether X sounds more like A or more like B.

Use headphones for best results. All 9 steps × 2 continua will be tested in random order.
